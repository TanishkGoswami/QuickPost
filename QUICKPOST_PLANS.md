## 2. Strategic Conversion Triggers (Free ➡️ Pro)

To ensure high conversion rates from Free to Paid, the Free plan has been optimized with three distinct barriers that do not hurt the initial user experience but make upgrading inevitable for growing accounts:

### ⚡ 1. The Branding Watermark (Viral Growth + Upgrade Hook)
*   **The Mechanism:** Every DM sent on the Free plan automatically appends:
    `_⚡ Automated via QuickPost.co (Get it Free)_`
*   **Why it converts:** Creators and brands want to look highly professional and premium. Showing a "free tool" watermark to their audience is a major pain point. They will upgrade to **Pro** to white-label their DMs.
*   **Viral Loop:** Every free user who goes viral sends thousands of DMs containing your signup link, driving free, compounding referral traffic to QuickPost.

### ⚡ 2. The 50 Replies/Month & 1 Trigger Word Limits
*   **The Mechanism:** Free users can only have **1 active trigger word** and **50 automated replies per month**.
*   **Why it converts:** 50 replies can be exhausted by a single successful Reel or post. Once the limit is hit, their automations pause. To keep capturing leads from their comments, they must upgrade. Gating it to 1 active trigger word also means they can't run automations on multiple posts simultaneously.

### ⚡ 3. Hinglish & Regional Language Trigger Matching
*   **The Mechanism:** Free accounts only match exact English keywords. Pro/Enterprise accounts use AI fuzzy-matching to understand Hinglish (e.g., *"link de bhai"*) and Hindi script comments.
*   **Why it converts:** Indian creators lose 40-60% of their potential leads on the Free plan because their audience comments in Hinglish. Upgrading to Pro instantly captures all these missed comments.

## 3. Plan Breakdowns

### 🟢 Free Plan (₹0)
*   **Channels:** 3 connected accounts (any mix).
*   **Scheduling:** Unlimited monthly posts, max 10 queued posts per channel.
*   **Auto-DM:** 1 active trigger word, 50 replies/month, and **QuickPost branding watermark** appended to all DMs. English-only trigger matching.

### 🔵 Pro Plan (₹999/mo)
*   **Channels:** Up to 10 connected accounts.
*   **Auto-DM:** Unlimited trigger words, unlimited replies, and **no watermarks (white-labeled)**. Hinglish and regional language matching enabled.

### 🟣 Enterprise Plan (₹2,999/mo)
*   **Channels:** Up to 30 connected accounts.
*   **Collaboration:** 10 team members, client-facing approval pipelines, white-labeled PDF analytics reports, developer API access, and VIP priority support.

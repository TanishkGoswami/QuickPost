# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

QuickPost is for creators, founders, agencies, and social media operators who manage many connected social accounts from one workspace. They need fast publishing, reliable account routing, accurate previews, queue visibility, and real platform feedback without switching between every native platform.

## Product Purpose

QuickPost helps users create, customize, schedule, publish, and review content across connected channels such as Instagram, YouTube, Threads, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Bluesky, Mastodon, Reddit, and Google Business. Success means the user can confidently see which account and platform a post went to, what happened after publishing, and what requires action.

## Positioning

A multi-account publishing control room that keeps platform-specific behavior honest while staying fast enough for daily content operations.

## Brand Personality

Calm, professional, operational. The interface should feel like a serious SaaS workspace: compact, clear, confident, and useful under pressure.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic marketing-page styling inside app surfaces, fake or mock analytics, decorative dashboard cards without workflow value, dark YouTube-clone styling, oversized empty states, crowded account switchers, and platform previews that do not match the real destination platform.

## Design Principles

- Show real platform state and avoid invented numbers or placeholder analytics.
- Make platform-wide filters and account-specific filters visually distinct.
- Prioritize dense, scannable layouts for repeated daily use.
- Preserve the existing QuickPost visual system in `design.md`: cream canvas, white work surfaces, charcoal type, restrained borders, and orange for primary action emphasis.
- Keep previews and publish controls platform-aware, especially for YouTube, Instagram, and Threads.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion-safe transitions, and responsive layouts that remain usable from mobile width through desktop dashboards.
